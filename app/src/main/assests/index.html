ABOUT US

Established in 2018, the Marshmellow cafe is an independent counter service cafe centrally located in Colombos most glamorous neighborhood, serving coffee-made with Sri Lanka’s premium mountain grown arabica beans.Our food that we serve, are American oriented portions, from chilli dogs to salads, but with an edge, which is our signature.Be sure to check out our social media pages and stay tuned for the latest happenings around us. See you at the Marshmellow Cafe !

Welcome to the official website of Marshmellow Cafe. Our amazing staff strive to carefully craft our drinks and food so you can experience happiness in every sip or bite. Walk in, order, collect and pull up a chair at any of our seven locations and be a part of a movement that exudes a fun, relaxing ambience for great company fuelled by delicious coffees and teas. Have a look at our diverse menus or browse our outlet finder to see which of our trendy coffee houses is closest to you. What are you waiting for? The Bean isn’t going to order itself. Smiles are free with every order.
